About

I am a Human Computer Interaction Student at the New Jersey Institute of Technology (NJIT) who enjoys designing for the sake of helping others and learning about how technology affects the daily lives of people. From my college courses, I gained experience conducting user research and developing practical prototypes based on my findings. Outside of my academics, I have interned at TD Bank AMCB as a Data Scientist, which has expanded my perspective of the software design process. Notable achievements of mine include placing first with a teammate in NJIT SIGCHI’s Design Jam 2025 as well as placing runner-up in TD Bank AMCB’s Early Talent Summer 2025 Intern Innovation Challenge.

Open to opportunities that promote ingenuity and collaboration, allowing me to expand my skills, knowledge, and perspective as a designer.
